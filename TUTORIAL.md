## 🎛️ Sidebar Controls

### Network Layer

### 🎯 Nodes in Focus — The Resolution Slider
Choose how many of the most-influential nodes to analyze:

| Resolution | Character | Best For |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **150**   | Focused core | Quick overview, sermon prep, sharing with others |
| **250**   | Emerging voices | Seeing peripheral concepts begin to cluster |
| **350**   | Fuller diversity | Mid-depth research, teaching preparation |
| **500**   | Complete topology | Deep research, discovering hidden structures |

**Pro tip**: The dominant axes (Divine-Consciousness, Incarnate-Process, Revelation-Spirit) are stable across resolutions — but entirely new axes (Jonah-Fish, Flesh-Christ, Wilderness-Bread) only emerge at 500 nodes.

### 🧘 Structural Contemplation (Node Exclusion)

This is the dashboard's **research superpower**. Remove nodes to see what hidden structures emerge beneath them.

**Three ways to exclude:**

1. **Quick-pick buttons** — click the top 5 most influential concepts (`god`, `jes`, `rev`, `jon`, `wil`) to toggle them on/off
2. **Curated presets** — pre-built theological experiments:
   - *🕊️ Silence the Dominant*: removes `@god`, `@jesus`, `@revelation`
   - *💬 Beyond the Prophets*: removes `@jonah`, `@the_prophet`, `@peter`, `@paul`
   - *🌱 Reveal Underlying (Top 5)*: removes the top 5 most-influential nodes
   - *📜 Strip Meta-Terms*: removes `@theology`, `@meditation`, `@mind`
   - *🔥 Kairos Minimal*: removes the top 10 concepts (maximum minimalism)
3. **Searchable multiselect** — type any node name to add it to exclusions

**🔄 Clear all exclusions** button resets everything.

When exclusions are active, a blue banner appears above the metrics row, and **every tab recomputes** to show the reduced topology.

## 🔥 The Kairos Pulse Card

Your one-glance theological read. Four columns:

| Field | What It Shows |
|-------|--------------|
| 🎯 **Central Axis** | The node with highest betweenness — the "semantic gravity well" |
| 👑 **Dominant Axis** | The community with the largest share of network weight |
| 🌱 **Emerging Axis** | The second-largest community — what's rising beneath the dominant |
| 🔗 **Strongest Bond** | The edge with highest weight — your deepest conceptual bond |

Below the columns, **pulse messages** interpret the topology:
- 🎯 *"Focused"* — concentrated around a few central concepts
- 🌐 *"Diversified"* — multiple distinct voices active
- ⚖️ *"Biased"* — one community dominates

## 📑 The Eight Analysis Tabs

### 🔝 Top Nodes
Ranked list of the 20 most-influential concepts by betweenness centrality.
- **Table**: exact BC values, degree, weighted degree, community
- **Chart**: horizontal bar chart colored by community

**Reading tip**: High BC = this concept bridges multiple conversations. `@god` at BC 0.92 means 92% of the network's bridging paths go through it.

### 🔗 Top Edges
The 20 strongest conceptual bonds — pairs of concepts that appear together most.
- Edge weight labels now display outside each bar for easy reading
- `@jonah ↔ @god` (weight 468) is your signature bond across all resolutions

### 🎨 Communities
The network's natural clusters, **auto-labeled** with theological names:
- *Divine-Consciousness Axis* (`@god`, `@jonah`, `@mind`)
- *Incarnate-Process Axis* (`@jesus`, `@wilderness`, `@leaven`)
- *Revelation-Spirit Axis* (`@revelation`, `spirit`, `@christ`)
- *Prayer-Scripture Axis* (`@prayer`, `@scripture`)
- ...plus auto-derived names for emergent clusters

Visual: cards with colored borders + a donut chart of weight distribution.

### 🌉 Structural Gaps
**Bridge opportunities** — pairs of communities that are weakly connected. Writing a statement that bridges concepts from both sides can produce novel theological insight.

Each gap card shows both cluster names and their top concepts on either side.

### 📊 Exclusion Impact *(New)*
A visual reference chart showing the **betweenness footprint** of the top 25 concepts.
- **Teal bars** = currently included in analysis
- **Red bars** = currently excluded
- Longer bars = more structural influence

Use this tab to **choose which nodes to exclude next**. If you see `@god` with BC 0.918 and `@jesus` with BC 0.148, excluding `@god` will have a much bigger impact.

### ⚖️ Compare *(New)*
Side-by-side **before/after comparison** when exclusions are active.
- **Full Network** metrics (left) vs **Reduced Topology** metrics (right) with delta indicators
- **Community Weight Shift** table showing which axes gained/lost influence (in percentage points)
- **Silenced vs Newly Visible** bonds — which top-10 edges disappeared and which rose

This is where the real revelation happens. When you exclude `@god`, you'll see:
- The Divine-Consciousness Axis shrinks
- Other axes proportionally grow
- Bonds like `@jonah ↔ @god` vanish from the top-10
- New bonds like `@word ↔ @christ` become visible

### 📜 Statements
All 6,734 source statements from your corpus with case-insensitive search.
Type any word to filter — great for tracing where a concept appears in your writings.

### 🧾 Raw JSON
Export the current view as JSON for research, sharing, or debugging.
- See the underlying data structure
- One-click download of the current resolution's JSON
- Shows modularity, diversity stats, top clusters

## 🧘 Contemplative Workflows

### Workflow 1: "What does the Spirit show beneath the dominant voice?"
1. Set resolution to **500** (maximum detail)
2. Quick-pick `@god` (or use preset "🕊️ Silence the Dominant")
3. Go to the **⚖️ Compare** tab

### Workflow 2: "What are my hidden theological centers?"
1. Set resolution to **500**
2. Apply preset "🔥 Kairos Minimal" (removes top 10 nodes)

1. Set resolution to **350**

3. Note which axes stayed stable vs. which transformed

2. Read the **🔥 Kairos Pulse Card** aloud

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Blank dashboard | JSON files missing | Check `data/` folder has `layer_1_*.json` files |
| "Unexpected data structure" | Cached stale data | Click *Clear cache* in Diagnostics |
| Iframe blank | InfraNodus down, or graph set to private | Click "Open in new tab" to verify |
| Metrics don't update | Streamlit cache | Click *Clear cache* in sidebar diagnostics |
| Exclusions don't persist | Session reset on redeploy | Exclusions are session-scoped by design |

## 🎓 Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Betweenness Centrality (BC)** | How often a node acts as a bridge on shortest paths between other nodes. High BC = high structural influence. |
| **Weighted Degree** | Total weight of edges connected to a node. High = much-discussed concept. |
| **Modularity** | How cleanly the network splits into communities. 0 = no structure; 1 = perfect silos. Yours: 0.22-0.30. |
| **Community** | A cluster of nodes more densely connected to each other than to the rest of the network. Auto-detected. |
| **Structural Gap** | A pair of communities with weak bridging. Opportunity for new synthesis. |
| **Diversity Score** | Focused / Diversified / Biased — characterizes the balance of voices in the network. |

## 🚀 Tips for Power Users

- **Resolution matters for gap analysis**: gaps at 150 nodes are shallow; gaps at 500 nodes reveal deeper structural opportunities.
- **Combine exclusions with resolution**: removing `@god` at 500 nodes is more revelatory than at 150.
- **The iframe is always live**: even when your dashboard shows a reduced topology, the iframe stays full. Use it for visual context.
- **Download the JSON** whenever you find a revealing view — timestamp it for your research archive.
- **The "Compare" tab is most powerful** right after applying a preset — read the Community Weight Shift table carefully.
